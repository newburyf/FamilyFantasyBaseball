Newbury Family Playoff Fantasy Baseball

Home Scores

Welcome to the Newbury family playoff fantasy baseball! To view scores, navigate to the scores page. During the playoffs scores will be updated daily.

Rules

Each round participants draft an infielder, outfielder, and a pitcher, draft picks carry over between rounds if the player's team isn't eliminated. Draft order is determined by previous year's result.
